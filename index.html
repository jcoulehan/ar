Mobile Web AR
"Easily accessible Augmented Reality"
WHAT IS THIS?
This is a hackathon project exploring the possibilities of Augmented Reality (AR) via a web browser on a mobile device.
HOW TO USE?
Note: These instructions assume you are not currently viewing this website on your phone.
Step 1 - Grab a phone*
*This should work on Android and iOS 11.
Step 2 - Focus your phone’s camera on the QR-code for one of the examples below.
If your phone is happy to read QR codes, it will open your web browser to an augmented reality page.
OR - Open your phone’s web browser and type in the URL for one of the examples below.
Step 3 - Please allow access to your phone's camera when prompted.
Step 4 - Point the phone at the AR marker (the square) for the same example.
You need to have the whole square in your phone’s viewer.
You are DONE! Enjoy!
